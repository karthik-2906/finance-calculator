What are fixed deposits (FD)?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a type of investment offered by banks and financial institutions where you deposit a lump sum amount for a fixed tenure at a predetermined interest rate. In return, the bank pays you interest on the principal amount over the chosen period, and at the end of the term, you get back your principal along with the accrued interest.
